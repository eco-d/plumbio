Projects

Below are the projects for PlumbI/O, seperated into categories based on what we think a solution may be. These are just suggestions, so feel free to solve any problem however you can. Click on the category to learn more about the projects!

Software

Headless Raspberry Pi Display
Short Term

Implement a solution so that after initial set-up, a user can simply boot a raspberry pi and have it display a specific website.

Order Tracking System
Short Term

Implement a solution so orders can be submitted to and tracked by logistics staff to improve delivery efficiency.

Plumber Siri
Short Term

A smart device like Siri / Alexa / Cortana, specifically for plumbing information.

Property Profiler
Long Term

Given a limited amount of information (address), compile property data from web sources (Zillow, County Auditor, etc..).

AI Training Customer
Long Term

Develop a system that takes in call data, develops a response system, and can be interacted with by plumbers to train them in field sales and customer interaction.

Hardware

Sound Activated On-Air System
Short Term

Develop a system that informs others outside of a room that phone calls are occurring inside the room.

Improve Warehouse Efficiency
Long Term

Develop a modified layout, new tool, or new process to improve warehouse use efficiency.

Improve Power Dolly
Long Term

Develop a new model or modify the existing model to improve ease of use.

Conceptual

Open Office Sound Dampening
Long Term

Develop a system to mitigate sound so that office personnel cannot be heard on calls they are not involved with.

Bio-friendly Yard Sign
Long Term

Develop a system so that yard signs benefit or at least do not harm the ecology of the yards they are placed in.

Recycle System for Job Waste
Long Term

Develop a system so that job site waste can be recycled or repurposed into either usable resources for other jobs or to be environmentally beneficial.

improve Water Testing
Long Term

Develop a system, tool, and/or process to improve water testing speed, ease, and/or accuracy.

High Level HQ Design
Long Term

Create conceptual art and/or designs for the new Ecoplumbers Headquarters.

Building Redesign
Long Term

Improve the layout of the current Ecoplumbers Headquarters to maximize efficiency and value to the employees.

Drain Cable Redesign
Long Term

Innovate Drain Cables to improve efficiency, ease of use, and/or durability.

Water Efficient Plumbing Fixtures/Systems
Long Term

Innovate Plumbing Fixtures or Systems to improve efficiency in water use.
