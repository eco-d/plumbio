When

3/20-3/21

8:00pm-8:00pm

Where

The Ecoplumbers HQ

4691 Northwest Parkway, Hilliard, Ohio 43026

Why

"Grow or die" has been a core value of the Ecoplumbers for over a decade. The Ecoplumbers is dedicated to supporting the next generation grow and innovate.

Prizes

The prize pool for this hackathon is divided between top ranked solutions, displays of creativity, and examples of innovation demonstrated by teams.

Food

Dinner on Friday and all Saturday meals will be provided to teams. Participants with dietary restrictions should list them during sign up.

Participants

Open to all majors. Any active member of Phi Rho or Triangle as well as close friends are welcome to register. Teams should be between 2 and 5 members with at least 1 active member of Phi Rho or Triangle on each team.
